# Account and Data Deletion

**Last Updated:** 20 May 2026

This page explains how users of the **Hanif Traders Technician** mobile
application, published by Hanif Traders, can request deletion of their
account and the data associated with it.

The Hanif Traders Technician app is an internal workforce application
available only to authorised employees of Hanif Traders. Accounts are
created and managed by Hanif Traders administrators; the app does not
support public self-registration.

---

## How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your account and your personal data, please send an
email to:

**[info@haniftraders.com](mailto:info@haniftraders.com)**

In your email, please include the following so we can locate your record:

1. Subject line: **"Account Deletion Request — Hanif Traders Technician"**
2. Your full name as recorded in our Employee records
3. The email address you used to sign in to the app
4. Confirmation that you wish your account and associated personal data
   to be deleted

We will acknowledge your request within **5 business days** and complete
the deletion within **30 days** of receipt, subject to the retention
requirements described below.

---

## What Will Be Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, we will delete or anonymise the
following data associated with your account:

- Your Mobile Access Request records (device identifiers and approval
  state).
- Your stored authentication credentials (API key and secret) on our
  backend.
- Your location history collected while you were on duty.
- Any free-text closing remarks attributed to you on customer complaint
  records will be anonymised (the remark text is retained as part of the
  customer record, but the author attribution is removed).

We will also revoke any active device access so the app on previously
authorised devices is signed out.

---

## What May Be Retained

Some information may be retained after account deletion, only to the
extent required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

- **Employee records** maintained for payroll, tax, employment, and
  compliance obligations are retained according to applicable Pakistani
  employment and tax law. This data is held in our HR system and is not
  used by the mobile app after deletion.
- **Customer complaint records** belong to Hanif Traders and our
  customers, not to the technician who serviced them; these records are
  retained but technician-author attribution is anonymised as described
  above.
- **Audit logs and system records** required for security, fraud
  prevention, or legal compliance may be retained for the periods
  prescribed by law.

After the retention period for each category lapses, the corresponding
records are deleted or anonymised.
