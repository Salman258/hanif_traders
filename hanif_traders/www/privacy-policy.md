# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 20 May 2026
**Last Updated:** 20 May 2026

Hanif Traders ("we", "us", "our") provides the **Hanif Traders Technician**
mobile application (the "App") as a workforce tool for authorised employees
of Hanif Traders. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App
collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

This App is intended solely for use by employees of Hanif Traders in the
course of their employment. It is not a public, consumer-facing service.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the information necessary to perform its function as
an internal field-service tool.

### 1.1 Account & Device Identification
- **Email address** — provided by you during initial access request. Used to
  match your account to an existing Employee record in our backend system.
- **Device identifier** — a randomly generated UUID stored locally on your
  device and shared with our backend so an administrator can approve
  access from your specific device.

### 1.2 Location Data
- **Foreground location (precise)** — collected while the App is open and
  used to support assignment routing.
- **Background location (precise)** — collected while you are on duty and
  the App is running in the background, used to record technician movement
  for the duration of your shift. Background location collection stops when
  you go off duty.

### 1.3 Authentication Credentials
- **API key and secret** — issued by our backend after an administrator
  approves your access. These credentials are stored on your device using
  the platform's encrypted secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android
  EncryptedSharedPreferences). They are transmitted only to the Hanif
  Traders backend over HTTPS.

### 1.4 Work-Related Data
- **Complaint records assigned to you**, including customer name, phone,
  address, complaint description, and your closing remarks. This data is
  fetched from the backend on demand.

The App does **not** collect: contacts, photos, microphone input, SMS,
calendar data, browsing history, or advertising identifiers.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

- Authenticating you and authorising access to your assigned work.
- Routing and assigning complaints to the appropriate technician.
- Recording location history during on-duty hours so management can verify
  visits and support payroll, dispute resolution, and operational planning.
- Allowing technicians to record closing remarks against completed
  complaints.
- Investigating operational incidents.

We do **not** use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose
unrelated to your employment.

---

## 3. How We Store & Protect Information

- Data in transit is protected via HTTPS / TLS.
- Authentication credentials on the device are stored in the platform's
  encrypted secure storage.
- Server-side data is stored on our backend hosted on Frappe Cloud and is
  accessible only to authorised Hanif Traders personnel.
- Access to backend data requires authentication and is governed by
  role-based permissions.

---

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third
parties. Information is accessible only to:

- You (your own data, via the App and the backend portal).
- Authorised Hanif Traders managers and administrators.
- Service providers strictly necessary to operate our backend (e.g. hosting
  providers), and only as required to provide that service.

We do not disclose data to third parties for marketing or analytics
purposes.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain employee account information, location history, and
work-related records for the duration of your employment with Hanif
Traders. Following the end of the employment relationship, data is
retained only as required by applicable law (e.g. tax, employment, or
audit obligations) and is otherwise deleted or anonymised.

You may request a copy of, or the deletion of, your personal data by
contacting us at the address below; we will honour the request to the
extent permitted by applicable law and our legal obligations.

---

## 6. Your Choices

- **Location permissions** — you can revoke location permissions through
  your device's settings at any time. Revoking background location will
  limit the App's ability to record on-duty movement and may affect your
  ability to use the App for its intended purpose.
- **Account access** — if you no longer require access to the App, contact
  your manager or write to us at the address below to have your access
  revoked and the App uninstalled.

---

## 7. Children

This App is not directed to children. It is intended only for employees of
Hanif Traders, all of whom are adults. We do not knowingly collect
information from children.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated"
date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Material changes
will be communicated through the App or by direct notice to employees.
